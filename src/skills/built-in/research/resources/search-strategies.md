# Search Strategies

## Query Formulation

### Basic Techniques
- Use specific, descriptive keywords
- Include relevant timeframes (e.g., "2024", "latest", "recent")
- Use quotes for exact phrases
- Add domain-specific terms

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine general and specific queries
- Search from different angles (technical, business, user perspective)
- Include alternative terms and synonyms

## Source Evaluation

### Credibility Indicators
- Author expertise and credentials
- Publication reputation
- Date of publication
- Citations and references
- Methodology transparency

### Red Flags
- No author attribution
- Outdated information
- Lack of sources
- Sensational language
- Single-source claims

## Research Depth Levels

### Quick Overview (2-3 sources)
- General search for main facts
- Suitable for simple questions

### Standard Research (5-7 sources)
- Multiple perspectives
- Fact verification
- Suitable for most queries

### Deep Research (10+ sources)
- Comprehensive coverage
- Expert sources
- Academic references
- Suitable for complex topics
